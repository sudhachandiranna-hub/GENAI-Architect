Thiru's Story — Why Manual Coding First

Thiru — NLP, Deep Learning & Machine Learning Background

When Thiru started in the field — working in NLP, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning — there was no ChatGPT. There was no GenAI. Everything was written by hand, manually, line by line.

2022 is when GenAI arrived. But it only became truly mature after 2023, once the SDE Benchmark was achieved — the point at which AI surpassed human-level performance in logical thinking and reasoning, especially in Python.

Only after that milestone did Thiru start using AI-assisted coding. That is the right order. Learn the foundation first. Then use AI as an amplifier.

— Thiru, GENAI Architect Tutor

💡 Thiru's Question to the Cohort

"Can we do AI-assisted coding now?"

Yes — we can. But before that, we need to understand the concepts well enough to know where to restrict, guide, and correct the AI in our code. Without concept clarity, you cannot use AI effectively or safely.
